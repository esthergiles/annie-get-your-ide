Grace Murray Hopper

Called the "Mother of Computing, Dr. Grace Hopper is considered one of the most influential women in computer science history for her work on programming languages and compilers which led toward creating modern programming languages.

Shafi Goldwasser

Shafi Goldwasser is a theoretical computer scientist and cryptography expert. Her career includes many landmark papers which founded entire subareas of CS. Dr. Goldwasser was awards the A.M. Turing Award in 2012.
